## PART 2 — 12 Tenses (structure yaad karo)

`V1` = base (play), `V2` = past (played), `V3` = past participle (played), `V-ing` = playing.

### Present
| Tense | Structure | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Simple Present | sub + V1/V5(+s) | He **plays** daily |
| Present Continuous | sub + is/am/are + V-ing | He **is playing** |
| Present Perfect | sub + has/have + V3 | He **has played** |
| Present Perfect Continuous | sub + has/have been + V-ing | He **has been playing** |

### Past
| Tense | Structure | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Simple Past | sub + V2 | He **played** |
| Past Continuous | sub + was/were + V-ing | He **was playing** |
| Past Perfect | sub + had + V3 | He **had played** |
| Past Perfect Continuous | sub + had been + V-ing | He **had been playing** |

### Future
| Tense | Structure | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Simple Future | sub + will + V1 | He **will play** |
| Future Continuous | sub + will be + V-ing | He **will be playing** |
| Future Perfect | sub + will have + V3 | He **will have played** |
| Future Perfect Continuous | sub + will have been + V-ing | He **will have been playing** |

**Important Rules:**
- Singular subject (He/She/It) → present me verb me **-s/-es** lagta hai (He goes).
- "Since" → time point (since 2020); "For" → duration (for 5 years) — Present Perfect ke saath.
- Universal truth/habit → **Simple Present** (Sun rises in the east).

## PART 4 — ✍️ Practice (Tense / Error)

Sahi verb form chuno:

1. She ______ (go/goes) to school every day.
2. They ______ (was/were) playing when it rained.
3. He has ______ (wrote/written) a letter.
4. The sun ______ (rise/rises) in the east.
5. I ______ (am/is) reading a book now.
6. We ______ (has/have) lived here since 2018.
7. One of the boys ______ (is/are) absent.
8. Neither he nor his friends ______ (was/were) present.

### ✅ Answers
1. **goes** 2. **were** 3. **written** 4. **rises** 5. **am** 6. **have** 7. **is** (one of + singular verb) 8. **were** (verb paas wale "friends" se match)
