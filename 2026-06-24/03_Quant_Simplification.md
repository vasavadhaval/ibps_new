# 🟡 Maths — Simplification (BODMAS) | Day 1

> Sabse EASY maths topic. Sirf order yaad rakho — koi heavy formula nahi.
> Yeh topic cut-off clear karne me sabse zyada madad karta hai.

---

## 1. BODMAS Rule (order yaad rakho)

| Letter | Matlab | Kya solve |
|---|---|---|
| **B** | Brackets | ( ), { }, [ ] pehle |
| **O** | Order | power / square root |
| **D** | Division | ÷ |
| **M** | Multiplication | × |
| **A** | Addition | + |
| **S** | Subtraction | − |

> **Important:** D aur M ek hi level par hote hain → jo **pehle (left se)** aaye wo karo. A aur S bhi same level.

---

## 2. Solved Examples

**Example 1:** `12 + 6 ÷ 2 × 3`
- Pehle ÷ : `6 ÷ 2 = 3`
- Fir × : `3 × 3 = 9`
- Fir + : `12 + 9 = ` **21** ✅

**Example 2:** `(8 + 2) × 5 − 4`
- Bracket: `8 + 2 = 10`
- ×: `10 × 5 = 50`
- −: `50 − 4 = ` **46** ✅

**Example 3:** `36 ÷ 6 + 4 × 2`
- ÷: `36 ÷ 6 = 6`
- ×: `4 × 2 = 8`
- +: `6 + 8 = ` **14** ✅

**Example 4 (square root):** `√144 + 5²`
- `√144 = 12`, `5² = 25`
- `12 + 25 = ` **37** ✅

## 4. Approximation (≈) — round figure banao

**Example:** `19.9 × 5.1 + 99`
- `19.9 ≈ 20`, `5.1 ≈ 5`, `99 ≈ 100`
- `20 × 5 + 100 = 100 + 100 = ` **≈ 200** ✅

> Approximation me exact answer nahi, **nearest option** chahiye hota hai — isliye round karo aur fast solve karo.
