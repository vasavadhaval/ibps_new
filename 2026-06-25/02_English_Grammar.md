## PART A — Subject-Verb Agreement (SVA)

**Rule ka core:** Verb hamesha **subject** se match kare — singular subject → singular verb, plural subject → plural verb.

### 5 Common Rules (yaad rakho)

1. **Singular subject → singular verb** (verb me 's/es' lagta hai).
   - ✅ He **plays** cricket. (He = singular)
   - ✅ They **play** cricket. (They = plural)

2. **"Each / Every / Everyone / Either / Neither" → hamesha SINGULAR verb.**
   - ✅ Each of the boys **is** present.
   - ✅ Every student **has** a book.

3. **Two subjects "and" se jude → PLURAL verb.**
   - ✅ Ram and Shyam **are** friends.
   - ⚠️ Lekin agar ek hi cheez ho: "Bread and butter **is** my breakfast." (ek unit)

4. **"Either...or / Neither...nor" → verb paas waale subject se match kare.**
   - ✅ Neither the teacher nor the students **were** there. (students = paas, plural)
   - ✅ Neither the students nor the teacher **was** there. (teacher = paas, singular)

5. **Uncountable nouns (water, information, advice, furniture) → SINGULAR verb.**
   - ✅ The information **is** correct. (informations ❌)

> **Trap:** "One of the + plural noun + singular verb." → ✅ One of the boys **is** absent.

## PART B — Articles (a / an / the)

### A vs AN (sound se decide, spelling se nahi)
- **a** → consonant *sound* se pehle: a boy, a university (yoo-sound), a one-rupee coin.
- **an** → vowel *sound* se pehle: an apple, an hour (h silent), an MBA (em-sound).

### THE (definite article) kab?
- Specific cheez: **the** sun, **the** moon, **the** Ganga.
- Pehle mention ho chuki cheez: "I saw a dog. **The** dog was black."
- Superlative ke saath: **the** best, **the** tallest.
- Rivers, seas, mountains range: **the** Himalayas, **the** Pacific.

> **No article:** general plural/uncountable — "Honesty is the best policy." "Dogs are loyal."

## ✍️ Practice — Error Detection (galti dhoondho)

1. Each of the students *have* submitted the form.
2. Ram and Shyam *is* good players.
3. He go to school daily.
4. She is an university student.
5. Neither of the boys *were* present.
6. The informations *are* useful.
7. I bought *a* umbrella.
8. One of the books *are* missing.
9. Honesty is *a* best policy.
10. My father *play* chess every evening.

---

## ✅ Answers

1. have → **has** (Each = singular)
2. is → **are** (Ram and Shyam = plural)
3. go → **goes** (He = singular)
4. an → **a** (university = "yoo" sound)
5. were → **was** (Neither = singular)
6. informations are → **information is** (uncountable)
7. a → **an** (umbrella = vowel sound)
8. are → **is** (One of the... = singular)
9. a → **the** (the best policy)
10. play → **plays** (My father = singular)
