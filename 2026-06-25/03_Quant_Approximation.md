> Kal BODMAS basics kiya. Aaj **Approximation** (round figure se fast solve) + speed drill.
> Yaad rakho: Quant sirf **cut-off + thode extra (8-12 marks)** ke liye. Easy hi karo.

---

## 1. Approximation ka Core Idea

Exact answer nahi chahiye — **nearest option** chahiye. Isliye numbers ko **round figure** me badlo, fir solve karo.

- `19.8 ≈ 20`, `48.9 ≈ 49`, `101.2 ≈ 100`
- `√? `, `%`, `×`, `÷` — sab approximate karke jaldi karo.

> **Rule:** Decimal `.5 se zyada` → upar round (49.7 → 50). `.5 se kam` → niche (49.2 → 49).

---

## 2. Solved Examples

**Example 1:** `19.9 × 5.1 + 99 = ?`
- `20 × 5 = 100`, `+ 100 = ` **≈ 200** ✅

**Example 2:** `√624 ÷ 4.9 = ?`
- `√625 = 25`, `25 ÷ 5 = ` **≈ 5** ✅

**Example 3:** `34.8% of 199 = ?`
- `35% of 200 = 70` → **≈ 70** ✅

**Example 4:** `(15.1)² − 99 = ?`
- `15² = 225`, `225 − 100 = ` **≈ 125** ✅

**Example 5:** `1199 ÷ 5.9 + 40.2 = ?`
- `1200 ÷ 6 = 200`, `+ 40 = ` **≈ 240** ✅
