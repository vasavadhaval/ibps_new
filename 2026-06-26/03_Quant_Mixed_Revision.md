## 1. 60-Second Recap

- **BODMAS:** Brackets → Order(power) → Division → Multiplication → Addition → Subtraction. (D-M ek level, A-S ek level — left se.)
- **Approximation:** numbers round karo, **nearest option** uthao. `.5+` upar, `.5−` niche.
- **Speed base:** Squares 1-20, Cubes 1-12, Fraction→% (1/2=50%, 1/4=25%, 1/8=12.5%...).

## ✅ Answers (solve karne ke BAAD)

**Set A:**
1. `36÷6=6`, `8×2=16`, `6+16−5=` **17**
2. `15+5=20`, `20×4=80`, `80÷8=` **10**
3. `√225=15`, `6²=36`, `15+36−10=` **41**
4. `40% of 250=100`, `100+12=` **112**
5. `6÷2=3`, `3×4=12`, `9+12=` **21**

**Set B:**
6. `30×3=90`, `90+49≈` **≈ 139**
7. `√289=17`, `17×4=` **≈ 68**
8. `20% of 500=` **≈ 100**
9. `1200÷6=` **≈ 200**
10. `11²=121`, `121−24=` **≈ 97**
