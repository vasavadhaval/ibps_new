## SECTION A — Error Detection (part dhoondho) (Q1-6)

1. (A) Each of the boys / (B) have / (C) a pen. / (D) No error
2. (A) She is good / (B) in / (C) English. / (D) No error
3. (A) He is married / (B) with / (C) a doctor. / (D) No error
4. (A) The Sun / (B) rise / (C) in the East. / (D) No error
5. (A) One of the / (B) student / (C) is absent. / (D) No error
6. (A) I prefer coffee / (B) than / (C) tea. / (D) No error

## SECTION B — Fill the Article (a/an/the) (Q7-10)

7. He is ____ honest man.
8. She is ____ university topper.
9. ____ Ganga is a holy river.
10. I saw ____ owl on the tree.

## SECTION C — Vocabulary (synonym choose) (Q11-15)

11. **Abundant** = (a) kam (b) bahut zyada (c) khaali
12. **Reluctant** = (a) khush (b) anman/hichkichaata (c) tej
13. **Frugal** = (a) fizool kharch (b) kifaayati (c) ameer
14. **Hostile** = (a) dost (b) virodhi (c) shaant
15. **Lucid** = (a) uljha (b) spasht (c) andhera

---

## ✅ ANSWER KEY (time ke baad)

1. **(B)** have → has (Each = singular)
2. **(B)** in → at (good **at**)
3. **(B)** with → to (married **to**)
4. **(B)** rise → rises (The Sun = singular)
5. **(B)** student → students (One of the + plural)
6. **(B)** than → to (prefer X **to** Y)
7. **an** (honest — h silent, vowel sound)
8. **a** (university — "yoo" sound)
9. **The** (Ganga — river)
10. **an** (owl — vowel sound)
11. **(b)** bahut zyada
12. **(b)** anman/hichkichaata
13. **(b)** kifaayati
14. **(b)** virodhi
15. **(b)** spasht
