# 🧠 Reasoning — Syllogism | Day 6

> Syllogism = "statements" diye jaate hain (All/Some/No), aur "conclusions" check karne hote hain ki **follow karte hain ya nahi**.
> Sabse safe tarika = **Venn diagram** banao. Prelims me 3-5 marks guaranteed.

---

## 1. 3 Basic Statements (ratta)

| Statement | Matlab | Venn diagram |
|---|---|---|
| **All A are B** | Saare A, B ke andar | A ka circle B ke andar |
| **Some A are B** | Kuch A, B hain | A aur B overlap (cross) |
| **No A is B** | Koi A, B nahi | A aur B alag-alag (no touch) |

---

## 2. Sabse Important Rules

1. **Venn diagram banao** — jaise statement kehti hai bilkul waise.
2. Conclusion tabhi **follow** karta hai jab wo **HAR possible diagram** me sach ho.
3. **"Possibility" / "Can be"** conclusions: jab tak diagram me wo **possible** ho, follow karta hai.
4. **Either-Or case:** do conclusions jo aapas me opposite hon (ek "Some are", dusra "No are" — same pair par), aur dono akele false ho → **Either-Or** sahi.

---

## 3. Quick Conclusion Table (yaad rakho)

| Statement | Definitely follows | Definitely NOT |
|---|---|---|
| All A are B | Some B are A ✅ | All B are A ❌ (zaroori nahi) |
| Some A are B | Some B are A ✅ | All A are B ❌ |
| No A is B | No B is A ✅ | Some A are B ❌ |
| All A are B + All B are C | All A are C ✅ | — |

> **Trick:** "All A are B" se **"Some B are A" hamesha sahi** (reverse some). Lekin "All B are A" kabhi pakka nahi.

---

## 4. Solved Examples

**Example 1:**
- Statements: All cats are dogs. All dogs are animals.
- Conclusions: (i) All cats are animals. (ii) Some animals are cats.
- Diagram: cats ⊂ dogs ⊂ animals.
- (i) **Follows** ✅ (cats animals ke andar). (ii) **Follows** ✅ (some animals = cats).

**Example 2:**
- Statements: Some pens are books. No book is red.
- Conclusions: (i) Some pens are not red. (ii) All pens are red.
- (i) **Follows** ✅ (jo pens books hain wo red nahi). (ii) **Does not follow** ❌.

**Example 3 (Either-Or):**
- Statements: Some A are B.
- Conclusions: (i) All A are B. (ii) Some A are not B.
- Dono akele pakka nahi, par milke saari possibility cover karte → **Either (i) or (ii)** ✅.

---

## 5. Solve karne ka Tarika (steps)

1. Har statement ka **Venn diagram** banao.
2. Diagram ko **alag-alag tareeke** se try karo (possibility check).
3. Conclusion **har** diagram me sach? → Follows. Warna → Does not follow.
4. Opposite pair + dono false → **Either-Or** check karo.

---

## 6. ✍️ Practice Questions

1. All books are pens. All pens are red. → (i) All books are red. (ii) Some red are books.
2. Some dogs are cats. All cats are animals. → (i) Some dogs are animals. (ii) All dogs are animals.
3. No man is woman. All women are humans. → (i) Some humans are not men. (ii) No man is human.
4. All apples are fruits. Some fruits are sweet. → (i) Some apples are sweet. (ii) All fruits are apples.
5. Some pens are pencils. No pencil is blue. → (i) Some pens are not blue. (ii) All pens are blue.
6. All A are B. No B is C. → (i) No A is C. (ii) Some A are C.
7. Some cars are buses. → (i) All cars are buses. (ii) Some cars are not buses.
8. All roses are flowers. Some flowers are beautiful. → (i) Some roses are beautiful. (ii) Some flowers are roses.
9. No cat is dog. Some dogs are pets. → (i) Some pets are not cats. (ii) All pets are dogs.
10. All teachers are smart. Some smart are rich. → (i) Some teachers are rich. (ii) All smart are teachers.

---

## ✅ Answers (solve karne ke BAAD)

1. (i) **Follows** ✅ (books⊂pens⊂red). (ii) **Follows** ✅
2. (i) **Follows** ✅ (cats⊂animals, some dogs=cats). (ii) **Does not follow** ❌
3. (i) **Follows** ✅. (ii) **Does not follow** ❌ (women humans hain, man bhi ho sakte)
4. (i) **Does not follow** ❌ (sweet fruits apples ho zaroori nahi). (ii) **Does not follow** ❌
5. (i) **Follows** ✅ (pencil pens hai aur blue nahi). (ii) **Does not follow** ❌
6. (i) **Follows** ✅ (A⊂B, B aur C alag → A,C alag). (ii) **Does not follow** ❌
7. **Either-Or** ✅ (dono milke saari possibility — Either (i) or (ii))
8. (i) **Does not follow** ❌. (ii) **Follows** ✅ (roses flowers hain)
9. (i) **Follows** ✅ (pets-dogs cat nahi ho sakte). (ii) **Does not follow** ❌
10. (i) **Does not follow** ❌ (rich smart teachers ho zaroori nahi). (ii) **Does not follow** ❌

---

> **Aaj ka takeaway:** Hamesha **Venn diagram** banao, "follows" tabhi jab **har** case me sach ho. "All A are B" → "Some B are A" pakka. Opposite pair dono false → **Either-Or**. Roz 10 syllogism karo, yeh easy + scoring hai.
