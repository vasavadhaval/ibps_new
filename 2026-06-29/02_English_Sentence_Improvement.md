# 📖 English — Sentence Improvement | Day 6

> Sentence Improvement = sentence ka ek part **bold/underline** hota hai, batana hai usko kis option se replace karein (ya "No improvement").
> Yeh Error Detection ka hi bada bhai hai — wahi grammar rules, bas yahan **sahi version** chunna hota hai.

---

## 1. Kaha-kaha Improvement maanga jaata hai (top patterns)

1. **Tense galat** → sahi tense se replace.
2. **Subject-Verb Agreement** → singular/plural verb fix.
3. **Preposition** galat → sahi combo.
4. **Article** missing/galat.
5. **Wrong word form** (adjective vs adverb): "He runs *quick*" → "*quickly*".
6. **Redundancy** (repeat words): "return *back*" → sirf "return".

---

## 2. Common Fix Examples

**(a) Tense:**
- ❌ If I *will* be rich, I will help. → ✅ If I **am** rich... (conditional me 'if' clause present)

**(b) SVA:**
- ❌ The quality of apples *are* good. → ✅ **is** good.

**(c) Preposition:**
- ❌ He is fond *to* music. → ✅ fond **of** music.

**(d) Adverb form:**
- ❌ She sings *beautiful*. → ✅ sings **beautifully**.

**(e) Redundancy (extra word hatao):**
- ❌ Repeat *again* / return *back* / *more* better → ✅ repeat / return / better.

**(f) Degree (comparison):**
- ❌ He is *more taller* than me. → ✅ **taller** (double comparative galat).

---

## 3. Redundancy Words (yeh aksar extra hote hain — hatao)
`return back, repeat again, more better, most best, two twins, free gift, final conclusion, advance planning, sufficient enough.`

---

## 4. ✍️ Practice — Sentence Improvement (bold part fix karo)

1. He **don't** like tea.
2. The list of students **are** ready.
3. She is married **with** a teacher.
4. He runs **more faster** than me.
5. Please **return back** my book.
6. If he **will** come, I will go.
7. She dances **graceful**.
8. He is junior **than** me.
9. One of my friends **are** a doctor.
10. He is the **most cleverest** boy.

---

## ✅ Answers

1. don't → **doesn't** (He = singular)
2. are → **is** (The list = singular)
3. with → **to** (married **to**)
4. more faster → **faster** (double comparative galat)
5. return back → **return** (back extra)
6. will → **comes/come** (if clause me present: "If he comes")
7. graceful → **gracefully** (adverb)
8. than → **to** (junior **to**)
9. are → **is** (One of... = singular)
10. most cleverest → **cleverest** (double superlative galat)
