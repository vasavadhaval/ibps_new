# 🧠 Reasoning — Direction & Distance | Day 7

> Direction & Distance = kisi person/object ki movement di hoti hai. Aapko final direction ya shortest distance nikalna hota hai.
> Yeh topic scoring hai, bas **diagram** sahi banana zaroori hai.

---

## 1. Basic Directions

```
        North
          ↑
West  ←   +   →  East
          ↓
        South
```

**Right turn / Left turn yaad rakho:**
- North face kar rahe ho → right = East, left = West
- East face kar rahe ho → right = South, left = North
- South face kar rahe ho → right = West, left = East
- West face kar rahe ho → right = North, left = South

---

## 2. Shortest Distance Formula

Agar movement ek side horizontal aur ek side vertical ban jaaye:

```
Shortest distance = √(a² + b²)
```

Example: 3 km east aur 4 km north → shortest distance = √(3²+4²) = √25 = **5 km**.

Common triples yaad rakho:
- 3-4-5
- 5-12-13
- 6-8-10
- 8-15-17

---

## 3. Solved Examples

**Example 1:** Rahul 5 km North gaya, phir right turn karke 3 km gaya. Ab wo starting point se kis direction me hai?
- North ke baad right = East.
- Final position: 5 km North + 3 km East.
- Direction = **North-East** ✅

**Example 2:** A person walks 4 km East, then turns left and walks 3 km. Shortest distance from start?
- East face karte hue left = North.
- Movement: 4 East + 3 North.
- Shortest distance = √(4²+3²)=5 km ✅

**Example 3:** Ramesh 6 km South gaya, right turn karke 8 km gaya. Start se distance?
- South face karte hue right = West.
- Movement: 6 South + 8 West.
- Distance = √(6²+8²)=10 km ✅

---

## 4. Solve Karne Ka Tarika

1. Rough diagram banao.
2. Start point mark karo.
3. Har movement ko arrow se draw karo.
4. Final position dekhkar direction likho.
5. Distance poocha ho toh horizontal/vertical net distance nikalo, fir formula lagao.

> **Tip:** Right/left turn current facing direction ke hisaab se hota hai, fixed map ke hisaab se nahi.

---

## 5. ✍️ Practice Questions

1. A walks 5 km North, then turns right and walks 4 km. Start se direction?
2. B walks 3 km East, then turns left and walks 4 km. Shortest distance?
3. C walks 6 km South, then turns left and walks 8 km. Start se distance?
4. D walks 10 km West, then turns right and walks 10 km. Start se direction?
5. E walks 5 km North, 5 km East, 5 km South. Start se kitna door?
6. F walks 7 km East, then 7 km West. Final position?
7. G faces North. He turns right, then left. Final direction?
8. H walks 12 km North and 5 km West. Shortest distance?
9. I walks 4 km South, 3 km East, then 4 km North. Start se direction/distance?
10. J faces East. He turns right, then right again. Final direction?

---

## ✅ Answers

1. North + East → **North-East**
2. 3 East + 4 North → √(9+16)=**5 km**
3. South face, left = East → 6 South + 8 East → √(36+64)=**10 km**
4. West + North → **North-West**
5. North 5 and South 5 cancel; East 5 left → **5 km East**
6. East 7 and West 7 cancel → **starting point**
7. North → right East → left North → **North**
8. √(12²+5²)=√169=**13 km**
9. South 4 and North 4 cancel; East 3 left → **3 km East**
10. East → right South → right West → **West**

---

> **Aaj ka takeaway:** Direction questions me diagram mandatory hai. Net North-South aur East-West movement alag karo, fir direction/distance nikaalo.
