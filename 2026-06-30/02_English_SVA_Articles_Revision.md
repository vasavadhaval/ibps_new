## PART A — Subject-Verb Agreement Quick Rules

1. **Singular subject → singular verb**
   - He **plays**. She **goes**.

2. **Plural subject → plural verb**
   - They **play**. Students **go**.

3. **Each / Every / Either / Neither → singular**
   - Each boy **has** a pen.
   - Neither answer **is** correct.

4. **One of the + plural noun + singular verb**
   - One of the students **is** absent.

5. **Subject ke beech me phrase aaye toh confuse mat ho**
   - The quality of apples **is** good.
   - Subject = quality, apples nahi.

---

## PART B — Articles Quick Rules

### A / An
- **a** consonant sound se pehle: a boy, a university, a one-rupee coin.
- **an** vowel sound se pehle: an apple, an hour, an MBA.

### The
- Specific cheez: the sun, the moon, the Ganga.
- Superlative: the best, the tallest.
- Pehle mention ho chuki cheez: I saw a dog. The dog was black.

### No Article
- General ideas: Honesty is important.
- General plural: Dogs are loyal.

## 1. ✍️ Practice — Error Detection

1. Each of the students **have** a notebook.
2. The quality of these mangoes **are** excellent.
3. She is **an** university student.
4. One of my friends **are** a doctor.
5. He is **a** honest officer.
6. The news **are** shocking.
7. Every player **were** ready.
8. He is **an** European citizen.
9. **The** honesty is a great virtue.
10. The list of names **are** final.

---

## ✅ Answers

1. have → **has** (Each = singular)
2. are → **is** (subject = quality)
3. an → **a** (university = yoo sound)
4. are → **is** (One of... = singular)
5. a → **an** (honest = vowel sound)
6. are → **is** (news = singular)
7. were → **was** (Every = singular)
8. an → **a** (European = yoo sound)
9. The honesty → **Honesty** (general idea, no article)
10. are → **is** (subject = list)
