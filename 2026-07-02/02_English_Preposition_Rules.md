## 1. Preposition kya hota hai?

Word jo noun/pronoun se pehle aata hai aur relation batata hai: place, time, direction, cause.

Examples: in, on, at, to, for, with, of, by, from.

## 3. Place & Time (basic)

**Place:**
- **at** — specific point: at home, at the door, at school.
- **in** — enclosed/large: in India, in the room.
- **on** — surface: on the table, on the road.

**Time:**
- **at** — exact time: at 5 pm, at night.
- **on** — day/date: on Monday, on 2nd July.
- **in** — month/year/duration: in July, in 2026, in 2 hours.
- **for** — duration: for 5 years.
- **since** — starting point: since 2020.
- **from** — starting point of movement/time: from Monday.

> **Trap:** "since five years" ❌ → **for** five years. Since = point (since 2020).

## 5. ✍️ Practice — Error Detection

1. (A) He is fond / (B) to / (C) music. / (D) No error
2. (A) She is senior / (B) than / (C) her brother. / (D) No error
3. (A) I am tired / (B) from / (C) waiting. / (D) No error
4. (A) He insisted / (B) to / (C) his point. / (D) No error
5. (A) She is different / (B) than / (C) her sister. / (D) No error

---

## ✅ Answers

**Fill in the blanks:**
1. **at**  2. **of**  3. **to**  4. **to**  5. **for**
6. **to**  7. **at**  8. **of**  9. **to**  10. **on**

**Error Detection:**
1. **(B)** to → **of** (fond of)
2. **(B)** than → **to** (senior to)
3. **(B)** from → **of** (tired of)
4. **(B)** to → **on** (insisted on)
5. **(B)** than → **from** (different from)
