# 🟡 Quant — Simple Interest (SI) | Day 9

> Simple Interest = sabse easy maths topic Week 3 me. **Ek formula** — cut-off ke liye perfect.
> Heavy compound interest / tricky cases abhi skip karo.

---

## 1. Formula (yaad rakho)

```
SI = (P × R × T) / 100
```

| Symbol | Matlab |
|---|---|
| **P** | Principal (mool dhan / jo amount invest/borrow kiya) |
| **R** | Rate of interest (% per year) |
| **T** | Time (**saal** me — months ho toh ÷12) |
| **SI** | Simple Interest |
| **Amount** | P + SI |

---

## 2. Time Conversion

- **Months → Years:** 6 months = 0.5 year, 18 months = 1.5 year.
- **Days → Years:** exam me usually saal me hi dete hain. Agar days: T = days/365.

---

## 3. Solved Examples

**Example 1:** P = 5000, R = 10%, T = 2 years. SI?
```
SI = (5000 × 10 × 2) / 100 = 1000
Amount = 5000 + 1000 = 6000 ✅
```

**Example 2:** P = 8000, R = 5%, T = 3 years. SI?
```
SI = (8000 × 5 × 3) / 100 = 1200 ✅
```

**Example 3:** SI = 600, P = 4000, R = 5%, T = ?
```
600 = (4000 × 5 × T) / 100
600 = 200T
T = 3 years ✅
```

**Example 4:** P = 10000, SI = 1500 in 3 years. Rate?
```
1500 = (10000 × R × 3) / 100
1500 = 300R
R = 5% ✅
```

**Example 5:** 6 months, P = 2000, R = 8%. SI?
```
T = 0.5 year
SI = (2000 × 8 × 0.5) / 100 = 80 ✅
```

---

## 4. Short Tricks

1. **Same P, R, T** → SI directly proportional.
2. **Double time** → double SI (P, R same).
3. **Rate 10%, 2 years** → SI = 20% of P (quick: 10×2/100 = 20% of P).

---

## 5. ✍️ Practice Questions

1. Find SI on Rs 6000 at 8% per annum for 2 years.
2. Find amount on Rs 5000 at 10% for 3 years.
3. SI on Rs 4000 at 5% for 4 years = ?
4. In how many years will Rs 2000 yield Rs 600 as SI at 10% p.a.?
5. At what rate will Rs 8000 give Rs 1600 SI in 2 years?
6. SI on Rs 3000 at 12% for 6 months = ?
7. P = ?, R = 10%, T = 2 years, SI = 500. Find P.
8. Rs 10000 amounts to Rs 11500 in 3 years at SI. Find rate.
9. SI on Rs 2500 at 6% for 2.5 years = ?
10. Which gives more SI in 2 years: Rs 5000 at 8% or Rs 4000 at 10%?

---

## ✅ Answers

1. SI = (6000×8×2)/100 = **960**
2. SI = (5000×10×3)/100 = 1500, Amount = **6500**
3. SI = (4000×5×4)/100 = **800**
4. 600 = (2000×10×T)/100 → T = **3 years**
5. 1600 = (8000×R×2)/100 → R = **10%**
6. T=0.5, SI = (3000×12×0.5)/100 = **180**
7. 500 = (P×10×2)/100 → P = **2500**
8. SI = 1500, 1500 = (10000×R×3)/100 → R = **5%**
9. SI = (2500×6×2.5)/100 = **375**
10. First: (5000×8×2)/100 = 800. Second: (4000×10×2)/100 = 800. **Equal**

---

> **Aaj ka takeaway:** SI = (P×R×T)/100. Time hamesha **saal** me convert karo. Amount = P + SI. Yeh topic 1-2 marks exam me almost pakka aata hai — formula ek baar yaad, practice se speed aa jaati hai.
