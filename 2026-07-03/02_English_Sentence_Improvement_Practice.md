---

## 2. ✍️ Practice — Improve the bold part

1. He **don't** like coffee.
2. She is good **in** dancing.
3. He is married **with** her sister.
4. He runs **more faster** than me.
5. Please **return back** the book.
6. If he **will** pass, he will celebrate.
7. She speaks **fluent** English.
8. He is senior **than** his boss.
9. One of the boys **were** absent.
10. He is the **most cleverest** student.
11. I have been waiting **since** two hours.
12. She is **an** European.
13. He did not **do** nothing.
14. The news **are** good.
15. He insisted **to** his point.

---

## ✅ Answers

1. don't → **doesn't**
2. in → **at**
3. with → **to**
4. more faster → **faster**
5. return back → **return**
6. will → **comes** (If he comes...)
7. fluent → **fluently**
8. than → **to** (senior to)
9. were → **was** (One of... = singular)
10. most cleverest → **cleverest**
11. since → **for** (duration)
12. an → **a** (European = yoo sound)
13. nothing → **anything**
14. are → **is** (news = singular)
15. to → **on** (insisted on)
