## SECTION A — Linear Seating (Q1-6)

**Puzzle 1:** A, B, C, D, E — 5 chairs left to right.
- A at left end. B 2nd from left. C immediate right of B. E at right end.

1. Who sits at position 3?
2. Who sits between A and C?
3. Who is at the right end?

**Puzzle 2:** P, Q, R, S — 4 chairs.
- P at left end. Q 2nd from right. R immediate left of Q.

4. Who sits at the right end?
5. Who sits between P and Q?
6. Position of R?

## SECTION B — Box/Floor (Q7-12)

**Floor puzzle:** A, B, C, D, E — 5 floors. Floor 1 = ground (bottom).
- C on floor 3. A immediately above C. E on top floor. D not on floor 1. B below D.

7. Who lives on floor 1?
8. Who lives on floor 4?
9. Floor of B?

**Box puzzle:** P, Q, R, S — 4 boxes. Box 1 = bottom.
- Q in box 3. P immediately below Q. S in top box. R in bottom box.

10. Which box has P?
11. Which box has R?
12. What is in box 2?
