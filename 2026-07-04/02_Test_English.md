## SECTION A — Preposition / Error (Q1-8)

1. (A) She is good / (B) in / (C) English. / (D) No error
2. (A) He is married / (B) with / (C) a doctor. / (D) No error
3. (A) I prefer tea / (B) than / (C) coffee. / (D) No error
4. (A) He is junior / (B) than / (C) me. / (D) No error
5. (A) She has been working / (B) here / (C) since five years. / (D) No error
6. (A) He is afraid / (B) from / (C) snakes. / (D) No error
7. Fill: He is ____ honest man. (a/an)
8. Fill: She is ____ university student. (a/an)

---

## SECTION B — Sentence Improvement (bold part fix) (Q9-15)

9. He **don't** like tea.
10. He runs **more faster** than me.
11. Please **return back** the book.
12. If he **will** come, I will go.
13. She dances **graceful**.
14. One of the boys **were** absent.
15. He is the **most cleverest** boy.

---

## ✅ ANSWER KEY

1. **(B)** in → at
2. **(B)** with → to
3. **(B)** than → to
4. **(B)** than → to
5. **(C)** since → for
6. **(B)** from → of
7. **an** (honest — vowel sound)
8. **a** (university — yoo sound)
9. don't → **doesn't**
10. more faster → **faster**
11. return back → **return**
12. will → **comes**
13. graceful → **gracefully**
14. were → **was**
15. most cleverest → **cleverest**
