# 📖 English — Cloze Test | Day 13

> Cloze Test = passage me **blanks** hote hain. Sahi word/phrase choose karna hota hai.
> Grammar + meaning + context — teeno check karo.

---

## 1. Cloze Test ka Tarika

1. **Poora passage ek baar padho** — topic samajh jao.
2. Har blank ke liye **pehle aur baad** wala sentence padho.
3. Grammar check: subject-verb, tense, preposition.
4. Meaning check: passage ka tone (formal/informal, positive/negative).
5. Option eliminate karo — jo grammar/meaning se fit na ho.

---

## 2. Common Blank Types

| Type | Kya dekho |
|---|---|
| **Article** | a/an/the |
| **Preposition** | in/on/at/to/for |
| **Conjunction** | and/but/because/although |
| **Verb tense** | past/present/future match |
| **Vocabulary** | context se closest meaning |

---

## 3. Solved Example

**Passage:** The government has (1)______ several steps to improve education. Many schools (2)______ lacking basic facilities. The new policy (3)______ focus on rural areas.

**Options:** (1) a) took b) taken c) taking d) take
(2) a) is b) are c) was d) were
(3) a) will b) would c) shall d) should

**Answers:**
1. **taken** — "has taken" present perfect ✓
2. **are** — "schools" plural ✓
3. **will** — future policy focus ✓

## 4. ✍️ Practice Cloze (choose best option)

**Passage:**

Reading is one of the most (1)______ habits a person can develop. It not only (2)______ knowledge but also improves vocabulary. Many students (3)______ reading because they find it boring. However, those who read regularly (4)______ better in exams. Parents should (5)______ children to read from an early age.

**Blanks:**
1. a) bad b) harmful c) useful d) useless
2. a) destroys b) gains c) loses d) ignores
3. a) avoid b) love c) enjoy d) prefer
4. a) perform b) fails c) ignore d) hate
5. a) discourage b) force c) encourage d) prevent

---

## 5. ✍️ Practice Cloze 2

**Passage:**

The Internet has (1)______ the way we communicate. People can now (2)______ with anyone across the world instantly. However, excessive use of social media (3)______ lead to health problems. It is important to (4)______ a balance between online and offline life. We should use technology (5)______.

**Blanks:**
1. a) unchanged b) transformed c) ignored d) rejected
2. a) connect b) disconnect c) avoid d) refuse
3. a) cannot b) can c) never d) won't
4. a) destroy b) maintain c) ignore d) reject
5. a) wisely b) foolishly c) rarely d) never

---

## ✅ Answers

**Cloze 1:**
1. **c) useful** — positive context
2. **b) gains** — reading gives knowledge
3. **a) avoid** — "because boring" = negative
4. **a) perform** — regular readers do better
5. **c) encourage** — parents should motivate

**Cloze 2:**
1. **b) transformed** — Internet changed communication
2. **a) connect** — communicate globally
3. **b) can** — possible negative effect
4. **b) maintain** — balance rakho
5. **a) wisely** — sensible use
