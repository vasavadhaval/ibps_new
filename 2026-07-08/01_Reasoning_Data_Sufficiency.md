# 🧠 Reasoning — Data Sufficiency | Day 15

> Data Sufficiency = ek question + do (ya teen) statements diye jaate hain.
> Aapko **answer nikaalna nahi** hai — sirf batana hai ki **data kaafi hai ya nahi** answer nikalne ke liye.

---

## 1. Standard Options (yaad rakho)

Do statements (I aur II) waale questions me common options:

| Option | Matlab |
|---|---|
| **A** | Sirf statement I akela kaafi hai (II nahi) |
| **B** | Sirf statement II akela kaafi hai (I nahi) |
| **C** | I ya II — koi ek akela kaafi hai (either alone) |
| **D** | Dono milke bhi kaafi nahi |
| **E** | Dono I aur II **saath me** chahiye (dono zaroori) |

> Exact wording exam me thoda alag ho sakti hai — **padhke** samjho.

---

## 2. Solve Karne Ka Tarika

1. Question samjho — **kya poocha** hai.
2. **Statement I akela** dekho — answer aa raha hai? (II ko dhyan me mat rakho)
3. Fir **statement II akela** dekho.
4. Agar dono akele fail → dono milake try karo.
5. Answer nikaalne ki zaroorat nahi — bas **sufficiency** check karo.

> **Trap:** Jaldi mat socho ki "answer nikal aaya". Confirm karo ki **ek hi unique answer** aa raha hai.

---

## 3. Solved Examples

**Example 1:** Ram ki age kitni hai?
- I. Ram apni behen se 5 saal bada hai.
- II. Ram ki behen 20 saal ki hai.
- I akela: behen ki age nahi pata → fail.
- II akela: Ram ki age nahi → fail.
- I + II: behen 20, Ram = 25 ✅
- **Answer: E (dono chahiye)**

**Example 2:** A, B, C me sabse lamba kaun?
- I. A > B.
- II. B > C.
- I akela: C ka pata nahi → fail.
- II akela: A ka pata nahi → fail.
- I + II: A > B > C → A sabse lamba ✅
- **Answer: E**

**Example 3:** X kaunsa din hai?
- I. Kal Sunday tha.
- II. Parso Saturday tha.
- I akela: kal Sunday → aaj Monday ✅ (akela kaafi)
- II akela: parso Saturday → kal Sunday → aaj Monday ✅ (akela kaafi)
- Dono alag-alag akele kaafi → **Answer: C (either alone)**

---

## 4. ✍️ Practice Questions (option A-E choose karo)

1. Q: Ram ka salary kitna hai?
   - I. Ram ki salary Shyam se 5000 zyada hai.
   - II. Shyam ki salary 20000 hai.

2. Q: Kaun sabse chhota hai — P, Q, R?
   - I. P > Q.
   - II. Q > R.

3. Q: Aaj kaunsa din hai?
   - I. Kal Monday tha.
   - II. Parso Sunday tha.

4. Q: Ek number kya hai?
   - I. Wo 9 aur 13 ke beech ka number hai.
   - II. Wo ek even number hai jo 10 aur 14 ke beech me hai.

5. Q: A ki age?
   - I. A, B se 3 saal chhota.
   - II. B, C se 2 saal bada, C = 25.

---

## ✅ Answers

1. **E** — dono chahiye (Shyam 20000, Ram 25000)
2. **E** — dono milke P>Q>R, R sabse chhota
3. **C** — dono akele aaj = Tuesday nikaal dete hain
4. **B** — II akela: 10 aur 14 ke beech even = 12 (unique) ✅. I akela: 9-13 ke beech = 10,11,12 (multiple) → fail. Isliye sirf II kaafi.
5. **E** — I + II: C=25, B=27, A=24

---

## 5. Important Tips

- **Answer value** yaad rakhne ki zaroorat nahi — sirf "unique answer milega ya nahi".
- Har statement **alag** (independent) check karo pehle.
- Overlap/assume mat karo — sirf jo likha hai wahi use karo.

---

> **Aaj ka takeaway:** Data Sufficiency me answer nahi, **sufficiency** check karo. Har statement akela dekho, phir dono milake. A/B/C/D/E options ratta maaro. Yeh easy + fast marks hain.
