# 📖 English — Reading Comprehension (RC) | Day 15

> RC = ek passage diya jaata hai, uske baad questions. Meaning + inference + vocabulary poocha jaata hai.
> Prelims me 5-10 marks — passage samajhna zaroori hai.

---

## 1. RC Solve Karne Ka Tarika

1. **Pehle questions padho** (2-3 seconds each) — pata chale kya dhoondhna hai.
2. Phir passage **tez padho** — main idea samjho.
3. Direct questions (fact) → passage me answer dhoondho.
4. Inference questions → passage se **logically nikaalo** (apni marzi nahi).
5. Vocabulary (synonym/antonym) → context se meaning.

---

## 2. Question Types

| Type | Kaise solve |
|---|---|
| **Direct/Factual** | Passage me exact line dhoondho |
| **Inference** | Passage ke basis par logical conclusion |
| **Main idea/Title** | Poora passage kis bare me — central theme |
| **Vocabulary** | Word ka context meaning (synonym/antonym) |
| **Tone** | Author ka attitude (positive/negative/neutral) |

---

## 3. Practice Passage

**Passage:**

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming the banking industry. Banks now use AI to detect fraud, answer customer queries through chatbots, and analyze large amounts of data quickly. This helps reduce human error and saves time. However, AI also raises concerns. Many people fear that automation will replace jobs. Experts argue that while some tasks will be automated, AI will also create new roles that require technical skills. Therefore, employees must upgrade their skills to stay relevant. Banks that adopt AI wisely will gain a competitive advantage, but those that ignore data security may face serious risks.

---

## 4. ✍️ Questions

1. What is the main idea of the passage?
   - a) AI is bad for banks
   - b) AI is transforming banking with benefits and concerns
   - c) All bank jobs will be lost
   - d) Chatbots are useless

2. According to the passage, how does AI help banks? (direct)
   - a) By increasing errors
   - b) By detecting fraud and saving time
   - c) By removing all employees
   - d) By reducing customers

3. What should employees do (as per passage)?
   - a) Quit jobs
   - b) Ignore AI
   - c) Upgrade their skills
   - d) Fear automation

4. What is a risk mentioned for banks?
   - a) Too many customers
   - b) Ignoring data security
   - c) Using chatbots
   - d) Hiring experts

5. The word "competitive advantage" means:
   - a) Losing to rivals
   - b) An edge over competitors
   - c) A disadvantage
   - d) Equal position

---

## ✅ Answers

1. **b)** AI is transforming banking with benefits and concerns (main idea)
2. **b)** Detecting fraud and saving time (direct — line 2)
3. **c)** Upgrade their skills (inference from "must upgrade")
4. **b)** Ignoring data security (direct — last line)
5. **b)** An edge over competitors (vocabulary)
