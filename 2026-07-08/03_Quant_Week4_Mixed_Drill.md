## 1. 60-Second Recap

**Profit & Loss:**
- Profit = SP − CP | Loss = CP − SP
- Profit% = (Profit/CP) × 100 (hamesha **CP** par)
- SP = CP × (1 ± %/100)

**Average:**
- Average = Sum / Count
- Sum = Average × Count
- Replacement: sum change karo, phir divide.

---

## 2. ✍️ Mixed Practice (timer: 15 min)

**Set A — Profit & Loss (Q1-5):**
1. CP = 400, SP = 500. Profit %?
2. CP = 800, Loss = 10%. SP?
3. SP = 660, Profit = 10%. CP?
4. CP = 250, SP = 300. Profit %?
5. Article sold at Rs 450 with 25% profit. CP?

**Set B — Average (Q6-10):**
6. Average of 5 numbers is 20. Sum?
7. Average of 10, 20, 30, 40, 50?
8. 5 students avg 60, 3 students avg 50. Other 2 avg?
9. Average of first 5 multiples of 6?
10. Class 40 students avg 35. 10 join with avg 45. New avg (50 students)?

---

## ✅ Answers (solve karne ke BAAD)

**Set A:**
1. Profit=100, (100/400)×100 = **25%**
2. 800 × 0.9 = **720**
3. 660/1.1 = **600**
4. Profit=50, (50/250)×100 = **20%**
5. 450/1.25 = **360**

**Set B:**
6. 20×5 = **100**
7. 150/5 = **30**
8. Total=300, first 3=150, rest 2=150/2 = **75**
9. 6,12,18,24,30 → 90/5 = **18**
10. (40×35 + 10×45)/50 = (1400+450)/50 = **37**
