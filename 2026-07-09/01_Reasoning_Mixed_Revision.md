## ✍️ Mixed Practice (10 Q — timer: 20 min)

**Q1 (Inequality):** `A > B ≥ C`. Conclusion: A > C — Follows or Does not follow?

**Q2 (Coding):** If `CAT = DBU` (+1 each), then `RAT = ?`

**Q3 (Alphanumeric):** Series: `P  3  R  @  7  K  $  9  M  2  #  T  5  W  &  Q` — Left se 6th element?

**Q4 (Syllogism):** All pens are books. All books are red. Conclusion: All pens are red — Follows?

**Q5 (Direction):** Walk 5 km North, turn right, walk 3 km. Final direction from start?

**Q6 (Blood Relation):** A is father of B. B is mother of C. How is A related to C?

**Q7 (Linear):** A,B,C,D,E — 5 chairs. A at left end. B 2nd from left. C immediate right of B. E at right end. Who at position 3?

**Q8 (Floor):** C on floor 3. A immediately above C. E on top (5 floors, floor 1 = ground). Who on floor 4?

**Q9 (Order):** Rank 12th from top, 28th from bottom. Total persons?

**Q10 (Data Sufficiency):** Ram's salary? I: Ram earns 5000 more than Shyam. II: Shyam earns 20000. Sufficient?

---

## ✅ Answers

1. **Follows** (A > B ≥ C → A > C)
2. **SBU** (R→S, A→B, T→U)
3. **K** (pos: P1, 32, R3, @4, 75, K6...)
4. **Follows**
5. **North-East**
6. **Grandfather**
7. **C** (A1-B2-C3-D4-E5)
8. **A** (B1-D2-C3-A4-E5)
9. **12 + 28 − 1 = 39**
10. **Yes — both needed** (E: both statements together)
