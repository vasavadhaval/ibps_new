# 📖 English — Fill in the Blanks | Day 16

> Fill in the Blanks = sentence me blank diya hota hai. Sahi word/phrase choose karna — grammar + vocabulary + context.

---

## 1. Solve Karne Ka Tarika

1. **Poora sentence padho** — tone positive/negative?
2. **Grammar check:** tense, subject-verb, preposition before/after blank.
3. **Options eliminate** — jo grammar ya meaning se fit na ho.
4. **Collocation** yaad rakho — "depend on", "good at", "interested in".

---

## 2. Common Patterns

| Pattern | Example |
|---|---|
| Preposition after verb | depend **on**, good **at**, married **to** |
| Article | a/an/the before noun |
| Conjunction | although, because, however |
| Vocabulary | context se closest meaning |
| Tense match | past sentence → past verb in blank |

---

## 3. ✍️ Practice — Choose best option

1. She is very good ___ mathematics.
   - a) in  b) at  c) on  d) for

2. Success depends ___ hard work.
   - a) in  b) at  c) on  d) for

3. He was ___ tired that he fell asleep immediately.
   - a) so  b) such  c) very  d) too

4. The manager asked him to ___ the report by evening.
   - a) submit  b) submission  c) submitted  d) submitting

5. ___ honest man is respected by everyone.
   - a) A  b) An  c) The  d) No article

6. She has been working here ___ five years.
   - a) since  b) for  c) from  d) by

7. He is not only intelligent ___ also hardworking.
   - a) and  b) but  c) or  d) yet

8. The news ___ very shocking.
   - a) are  b) is  c) were  d) have

9. I prefer tea ___ coffee.
   - a) than  b) to  c) over  d) from

10. He is junior ___ me by two years.
    - a) than  b) to  c) from  d) over

---

## 4. ✍️ Practice — Vocabulary Blanks

11. The project was ___ due to lack of funds. (stopped/cancelled)
    - a) abandoned  b) enhanced  c) adopted  d) allocated

12. She gave a ___ explanation that everyone understood. (clear)
    - a) vague  b) lucid  c) hostile  d) trivial

13. The company needs to ___ its cybersecurity measures. (improve)
    - a) deteriorate  b) hinder  c) strengthen  d) abandon

14. He was ___ to take the risk. (unwilling)
    - a) eager  b) reluctant  c) jubilant  d) diligent

15. Data ___ means data remains accurate and unchanged. (correctness)
    - a) redundancy  b) integrity  c) fragmentation  d) breach

---

## ✅ Answers

1. **b) at** (good at)
2. **c) on** (depend on)
3. **a) so** (so...that)
4. **a) submit** (verb needed)
5. **b) An** (honest — vowel sound)
6. **b) for** (duration)
7. **b) but** (not only...but also)
8. **b) is** (news = singular)
9. **b) to** (prefer X to Y)
10. **b) to** (junior to)
11. **a) abandoned**
12. **b) lucid**
13. **c) strengthen**
14. **b) reluctant**
15. **b) integrity**
