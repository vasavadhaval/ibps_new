## 1. Quick Formulas

**Simple Interest:**
```
SI = (P × R × T) / 100
Amount = P + SI
Time in YEARS (6 months = 0.5)
```

**Percentage:**
```
x% of N = (x/100) × N
x% more than B → A = B × (1 + x/100)
x% less than B → A = B × (1 − x/100)
```

**Quick:** 10% = ÷10 | 20% = ÷5 | 25% = ÷4 | 50% = ÷2

## 2. ✍️ Practice (timer: 12 min)

**SI (Q1-5):**
1. SI on Rs 5000 at 10% for 2 years = ?
2. Amount on Rs 4000 at 8% for 3 years = ?
3. SI on Rs 3000 at 12% for 6 months = ?
4. In how many years will Rs 2000 yield Rs 600 SI at 10%?
5. P = ?, R = 8%, T = 2 years, SI = 640. Find P.

**Percentage (Q6-10):**
6. Find 25% of 800.
7. 45 is what % of 180?
8. A number increased by 20%. Original = 500. New value?
9. A is 25% more than B. B = 400. Find A.
10. Price 1000 decreased by 10%. New price = ?

---

## ✅ Answers

1. (5000×10×2)/100 = **1000**
2. SI=960, Amount = **4960**
3. T=0.5, (3000×12×0.5)/100 = **180**
4. 600 = (2000×10×T)/100 → T = **3 years**
5. 640 = (P×8×2)/100 → P = **4000**
6. **200**
7. (45/180)×100 = **25%**
8. 500×1.2 = **600**
9. 400×1.25 = **500**
10. 1000×0.9 = **900**
