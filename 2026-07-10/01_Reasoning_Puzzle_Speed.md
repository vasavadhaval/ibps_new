# 🧠 Reasoning — Puzzle Speed Practice | Day 17

> Aaj focus = **speed**. Linear + Floor puzzles timed solve karo.
> Prelims me 25 Q, 20 min — puzzles me zyada time mat do. **8-10 min per puzzle** target.

---

## 1. Speed Rules

1. **Fixed clue pehle** — ends, exact position, opposite.
2. Diagram **zaroor** banao — bina diagram mat karo.
3. Stuck ho 2 min se zyada → **chhod do**, baad me aao (exam strategy).
4. Position numbers likho — left/right confuse mat karo.

---

## 2. ✍️ Timed Puzzle 1 (target: 8 min)

**A, B, C, D, E** — 5 chairs left to right.

1. E sits at the right end.
2. A sits 2nd from the left.
3. B sits between A and C.
4. D is not at any end.

**Questions:**
- (a) Who sits at the left end?
- (b) Who is at position 4?
- (c) Who sits immediate left of C?

---

## 3. ✍️ Timed Puzzle 2 (target: 8 min)

**P, Q, R, S, T** — 5 floors. Floor 1 = ground (bottom).

1. T lives on the top floor.
2. P lives on floor 2.
3. Q lives immediately above P.
4. R does not live on floor 1.
5. S lives below R.

**Questions:**
- (a) Who lives on floor 1?
- (b) Who lives on floor 4?
- (c) Floor of S?

---

## 4. ✍️ Quick Fire (target: 4 min — no diagram needed)

6. Rank 15th from top, 25th from bottom. Total?
7. A > B > C. Who is shortest?
8. Walk 4 km East, 3 km North. Shortest distance from start?

---

## ✅ Answers

**Puzzle 1:** C(1) — A(2) — B(3) — D(4) — E(5)
- (a) **C** (left end)
- (b) **D** (position 4)
- (c) **B** (immediate left of C at pos 3)

**Puzzle 2:** S(1) — P(2) — Q(3) — R(4) — T(5)
- R not floor 1 → R at 4. S below R → S at 1. P=2, Q=3, T=5.
- (a) **S** (floor 1)
- (b) **R** (floor 4)
- (c) S at floor **1**

**Quick Fire:**
6. 15 + 25 − 1 = **39**
7. **C**
8. √(16+9) = **5 km**
