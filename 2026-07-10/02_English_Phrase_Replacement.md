# 📖 English — Phrase Replacement | Day 17

> Phrase Replacement = sentence me ek **phrase underlined/bold** hota hai. Sahi replacement choose karna hai.
> Error Detection / Sentence Improvement jaisa — grammar + redundancy + word form.

---

## 1. Common Replacement Patterns

| Galat phrase | Sahi replacement |
|---|---|
| return back | return |
| more better | better |
| most cleverest | cleverest |
| since 5 years | for 5 years |
| married with | married to |
| good in | good at |
| prefer X than Y | prefer X to Y |
| one of the boys were | one of the boys was |
| He don't | He doesn't |

---

## 2. ✍️ Practice — Best replacement for bold phrase

1. He **don't like** tea.
   - a) doesn't like  b) didn't liked  c) not like  d) no change

2. She is **good in** English.
   - a) good at  b) good on  c) good for  d) no change

3. Please **return back** my book.
   - a) return  b) give back  c) take back  d) no change

4. He runs **more faster** than me.
   - a) faster  b) more fast  c) fastest  d) no change

5. I have been waiting **since** two hours.
   - a) for  b) from  c) by  d) no change

6. He is **junior than** me.
   - a) junior to  b) junior from  c) junior over  d) no change

7. **One of the students were** absent.
   - a) was  b) are  c) have been  d) no change

8. He is **an European**.
   - a) a European  b) the European  c) one European  d) no change

9. She speaks **fluent** English.
   - a) fluently  b) fluency  c) fluentness  d) no change

10. Honesty is **a best** policy.
    - a) the best  b) best  c) a better  d) no change

---

## ✅ Answers

1. **a) doesn't like**
2. **a) good at**
3. **a) return** (back redundant)
4. **a) faster**
5. **a) for**
6. **a) junior to**
7. **a) was**
8. **a) a European**
9. **a) fluently**
10. **a) the best**
