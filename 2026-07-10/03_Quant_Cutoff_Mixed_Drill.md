## 1. Quick Recap

**Simplification:** BODMAS — Brackets → Order → ÷ → × → + → −

**Number Series:** Difference nikaalo → constant / increasing / ratio / square / cube

**Quadratic:** Roots nikaalo, compare x vs y. Mixed → cannot be determined.

---

## 2. ✍️ Mixed Practice (timer: 15 min)

**Simplification (Q1-3):**
1. `36 ÷ 6 + 8 × 2 − 5`
2. `40% of 250 + 12`
3. `(15 + 5) × 4 ÷ 8`

**Number Series (Q4-6):**
4. `5, 10, 20, 40, ?`
5. `2, 6, 12, 20, 30, ?`
6. `1, 4, 9, 16, 25, ?`

**Quadratic — Compare x and y (Q7-10):**
7. x² − 5x + 6 = 0; y² − 9y + 20 = 0
8. x² − 4x + 4 = 0; y² − 4y + 4 = 0
9. x² − 3x + 2 = 0; y² − 7y + 12 = 0
10. x² − 5x + 6 = 0; y² − 5y + 4 = 0

---

## ✅ Answers

1. 6+16−5 = **17**
2. 100+12 = **112**
3. 20×4÷8 = **10**
4. ×2 → **80**
5. diff +4,+6,+8,+10 → 30+12 = **42**
6. n² → 6² = **36**
7. x=2,3; y=4,5 → **x < y**
8. x=2,2; y=2,2 → **x = y**
9. x=1,2; y=3,4 → **x < y**
10. x=2,3; y=1,4 → **cannot be determined**
