# 📖 English — Reading Comprehension | Day 20

> Week 5 English focus: **RC practice**. Prelims me 5-8 marks RC se aate hain.
> Method: pehle questions → phir passage → direct/inference alag.

---

## 1. Quick Method

1. Questions pehle padho (30 sec).
2. Passage tez padho — main idea note.
3. Direct Q = line dhoondho.
4. Inference = logically nikaalo (apni baat mat ghusao).
5. Vocab = context se meaning.

---

## 2. Practice Passage

**Passage:**

Cybersecurity has become a critical concern for banks in India. With the rise of digital banking, customers transfer money, pay bills, and check balances online. This convenience also brings risks. Hackers target banking systems to steal personal data and money. Banks invest heavily in firewalls, encryption, and multi-factor authentication to protect users. Customers also have a role — they must not share OTPs, use strong passwords, and avoid clicking on suspicious links. Experts say that cybersecurity is a shared responsibility. If banks and customers work together, digital banking can remain both convenient and safe.

---

## 3. ✍️ Questions

1. What is the main idea of the passage?
   - a) Digital banking is useless
   - b) Cybersecurity is important for banks and customers
   - c) OTPs should be shared
   - d) Hackers are always successful

2. According to the passage, what do hackers target?
   - a) Only ATMs
   - b) Banking systems for data and money
   - c) Only passwords
   - d) Newspapers

3. What should customers NOT do?
   - a) Use strong passwords
   - b) Share OTPs
   - c) Avoid suspicious links
   - d) Check balances online

4. "Shared responsibility" means:
   - a) Only banks are responsible
   - b) Only customers are responsible
   - c) Both banks and customers must act
   - d) Government alone

5. Which security measure is mentioned?
   - a) Firewalls and encryption
   - b) Free loans
   - c) Longer working hours
   - d) More branches

6. Synonym of **critical** (as used here):
   - a) Unimportant  b) Crucial/important  c) Funny  d) Slow

---

## ✅ Answers

1. **b)** Cybersecurity important for banks and customers
2. **b)** Banking systems for data and money
3. **b)** Share OTPs
4. **c)** Both banks and customers must act
5. **a)** Firewalls and encryption
6. **b)** Crucial/important
