## 2. ✍️ Puzzle 1 — Linear (target: 8 min)

**A, B, C, D, E** — 5 chairs left to right.
1. B sits at the left end.
2. E sits 2nd from the right.
3. C sits immediate right of B.
4. D is not at any end.

**Questions:**
- (a) Who sits at the right end?
- (b) Who sits between C and E?
- (c) Position of A?

---

## 3. ✍️ Puzzle 2 — Floor (target: 8 min)

**P, Q, R, S, T** — 5 floors. Floor 1 = ground.
1. Q lives on floor 3.
2. P lives immediately below Q.
3. T lives on the top floor.
4. R does not live on floor 1.
5. S lives below R.

**Questions:**
- (a) Who on floor 1?
- (b) Who on floor 4?
- (c) Floor of S?

---

## 4. ✍️ Quick Fire (4 min)

6. Rank 10th from top, 30th from bottom. Total?
7. A taller than B, B taller than C. Tallest?
8. 6 km South, right turn, 8 km. Distance from start?

---

## ✅ Answers

**Puzzle 1:** B(1) — C(2) — D(3) — E(4) — A(5)
- E 2nd from right → pos 4. B left end. C right of B → pos 2. D not end → pos 3. A at 5.
- (a) **A**  (b) **D**  (c) Position **5**

**Puzzle 2:** S(1) — P(2) — Q(3) — R(4) — T(5)
- Q=3, P below=2, T=5. R not 1 → R=4, S=1 below R.
- (a) **S**  (b) **R**  (c) Floor **1**

**Quick Fire:**
6. 10+30−1 = **39**
7. **A**
8. √(36+64) = **10 km** (South then right=West)
