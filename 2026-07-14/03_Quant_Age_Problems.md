# 🟡 Quant — Age Problems | Day 21

> Age problems = present / past / future ages. Aksar **ratio** se jude hote hain.
> Easy formula + diagram se solve.

---

## 1. Core Idea

```
Present age → Past: age − n years
Present age → Future: age + n years

If ratio of ages A:B = m:n now:
After t years: (A+t):(B+t)
t years ago: (A−t):(B−t)
```

---

## 2. Solved Examples

**Example 1:** A is 20, B is 30. Ratio of ages after 5 years?
```
A=25, B=35 → 25:35 = 5:7 ✅
```

**Example 2:** Present age ratio A:B = 4:5. Sum = 45. Ages?
```
4+5=9 parts. One part = 5. A=20, B=25 ✅
```

**Example 3:** Father is thrice son. After 12 years, father will be twice son. Find ages?
```
Let son = x, father = 3x
After 12: 3x+12 = 2(x+12)
3x+12 = 2x+24 → x = 12
Son=12, Father=36 ✅
```

**Example 4:** A is 5 years older than B. Sum of ages = 35. Ages?
```
B = x, A = x+5
2x+5 = 35 → 2x = 30 → x=15
B=15, A=20 ✅
```

**Example 5:** 5 years ago ratio A:B = 2:3. Present A=15. Present B?
```
5 years ago A=10. Ratio 2:3 → B ago = 15. Present B=20 ✅
```

## 3. ✍️ Practice

4. A is 6 years older than B. Sum=42. Age of B?
5. 4 years ago A:B = 1:2. A present=12. B present?
6. Ratio A:B = 5:7. Diff=8. Ages?
7. Mother is twice daughter. After 10 years mother will be 1.5 times daughter. Daughter now?
8. Sum of ages of A and B = 50. A is 10 older. Ages?

## ✅ Answers

1. 24:30 = **4:5**
2. A = 3/8×40 = **15**
3. Son=x, Father=4x. 4x+8=3(x+8) → 4x+8=3x+24 → x=**16**. Father=64
4. B=x, A=x+6. 2x+6=42 → x=**18**
5. 4 yrs ago A=8. Ratio1:2 → B ago=16. Present B=**20**
6. Diff parts=2=8 → part=4. A=20, B=**28**
7. Daughter=x, Mother=2x. 2x+10=1.5(x+10) → 2x+10=1.5x+15 → 0.5x=5 → x=**10**. Mother=20
8. A=30, B=**20**
