**Q2 (Coding):** PEN = QFO (+1). MAP = ?

**Q3 (Syllogism):** All cats are animals. Some animals are pets. Conclusion: Some cats are pets — Follows?

**Q4 (Direction):** 6 km East, turn left, 8 km. Shortest distance?

**Q6 (Order):** Rank 8th from top, 22nd from bottom. Total?

**Q12 (Circular):** A, B, C, D — 4 persons, face centre. A and C opposite. B immediate right of A. Who sits opposite B?
