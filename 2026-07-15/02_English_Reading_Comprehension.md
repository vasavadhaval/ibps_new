> Week 5 RC practice continue. Banking + technology passage.

## Passage

Digital payments have grown rapidly in India after UPI was introduced. Customers can transfer money instantly using mobile apps without visiting a bank branch. This has reduced cash handling and improved transparency. However, fraud through fake links and phishing messages has also increased. Banks warn customers never to share OTP or PIN with anyone. The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) regulates payment systems to ensure safety. Financial literacy among users is as important as technology upgrades by banks.

---

## ✍️ Questions

1. Main idea?
   - a) UPI is harmful  b) Digital payments grew but need safety awareness  c) Cash is better  d) RBI does not regulate

2. What reduced after digital payments?
   - a) Transparency  b) Cash handling (at branches)  c) Mobile phones  d) Banks

3. What should customers NOT share?
   - a) Name  b) OTP/PIN  c) Address  d) Account number only

4. Who regulates payment systems?
   - a) ISRO  b) RBI  c) Police only  d) No one

5. "Phishing" in context means:
   - a) Fishing sport  b) Fraudulent trick to steal info  c) Payment method  d) Bank branch

6. Inference: Financial literacy is important because:
   - a) Users must avoid fraud traps  b) Banks should close  c) UPI should stop  d) Cash returns

---

## ✅ Answers

1. **b**
2. **b**
3. **b**
4. **b**
5. **b**
6. **a**
