# 🟡 Quant — Age + Ratio Mixed Drill | Day 22

> Week 5 maths: Age + Ratio dono practice. Target **7+ / 10** in 12 min.

---

## Quick Recap

**Ratio:** a:b = m:n → parts method, cross multiply  
**Age:** present x, past x−t, future x+t. Father-son equations common.

---

## ✍️ Practice

1. A:B = 3:4. If A = 15, B = ?
2. Divide Rs 700 in ratio 2:5. Larger share?
3. Present ratio A:B = 5:7. Sum = 48. Age of B?
4. A is 4 years older than B. Sum = 36. Age of A?
5. 5 years ago ratio A:B = 2:3. A present = 17. B present?
6. Father is 3 times son. After 10 years, twice son. Son's age now?
7. If 2:5 = 8:x, find x.
8. A:B = 4:5, B:C = 5:6. A:C = ?
9. Ratio boys:girls = 7:5. Total 60. Girls?
10. Diff of ages A and B is 6. Ratio A:B = 5:4. Ages?

---

## ✅ Answers

1. B = 15×4/3 = **20**
2. Larger = 5/7 × 700 = **500**
3. B = 7/12 × 48 = **28**
4. B=x, A=x+4. 2x+4=36 → x=16, A=**20**
5. 5 yrs ago A=12. Ratio 2:3 → B ago=18. Present B=**23**
6. Son=x, Father=3x. 3x+10=2(x+10) → x=**10**
7. x = 5×8/2 = **20**
8. A:B=4:5, B:C=5:6 → A:C = **4:6 = 2:3**
9. Girls = 5/12 × 60 = **25**
10. Diff parts=1=6 → part=6. A=30, B=**24**
