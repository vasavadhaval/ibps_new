## Passage

Artificial intelligence is being used by banks to detect unusual transactions and prevent fraud. Machine learning models analyze patterns in customer behaviour and flag suspicious activity in real time. This helps reduce false alarms while catching genuine threats. However, AI systems need large amounts of clean data to work well. Biased or incomplete data can lead to wrong decisions. Regulators expect banks to explain how AI models make decisions, especially when customers are affected. Human oversight remains essential — technology should support bankers, not replace judgment entirely.

6. Synonym of **flag** (here):
