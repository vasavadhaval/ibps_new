## ✍️ Practice

**Ratio (Q1-4):**
1. A:B = 4:7. If B = 35, A = ?
2. Divide Rs 900 in ratio 2:3:4. Largest share?
3. A:B = 3:5, B:C = 5:8. A:C = ?
4. Boys:girls = 5:4. Total 54. Boys = ?

**Age (Q5-8):**
5. A is 6 years older than B. Sum = 40. Age of B?
6. Present ratio A:B = 2:3. Sum = 50. Age of A?
7. Father = 3 × son. After 12 years, 2 × son. Son now?
8. 4 years ago A:B = 1:3. A present = 14. B present?

**Quick (Q9-12):**
9. 25% of 480 = ?
10. SI on Rs 4000 at 5% for 2 years = ?
11. 36 ÷ 6 + 8 × 2 − 5 = ?
12. CP = 500, SP = 600. Profit %?

---

## ✅ Answers

1. A = 4/7 × 35 = **20**
2. Largest = 4/9 × 900 = **400**
3. A:B=3:5, B:C=5:8 → A:C = **3:8**
4. Boys = 5/9 × 54 = **30**
5. B=x, A=x+6. 2x+6=40 → x=17. B=**17**
6. A = 2/5 × 50 = **20**
7. 3x+12=2(x+12) → x=**12**
8. 4 yrs ago A=10. Ratio 1:3 → B ago=30. Present B=**34**
9. **120**
10. (4000×5×2)/100 = **400**
11. 6+16−5 = **17**
12. Profit 100, **20%**
