# 🧠 Reasoning — Puzzle Timed Mixed | Day 24

> Week 5 speed day. **2 puzzles — 8 min each max.** Stuck > 2 min → skip, baad me aao.

## 1. Speed Checklist
1. Positions number karo (1–5 ya N/E/S/W).
2. Fixed clue pehle (ends, exact floor, opposite).
3. Diagram bina mat solve karo.
4. Exam: 25 Q / 20 min — puzzle pe zyada time mat do.

---

## 2. ✍️ Puzzle 1 — Circular (target: 8 min)

**P, Q, R, S, T** — 5 persons sit around a **circular table** facing **centre**.

1. Q sits immediate left of P.
2. R sits opposite Q.
3. S sits between R and T (clockwise from R to T).
4. T is not neighbour of P.

**Questions:**
- (a) Who sits opposite P?
- (b) Who is immediate right of Q?
- (c) Who is neighbour of S?

---

## 3. ✍️ Puzzle 2 — Floor (target: 8 min)

**P, Q, R, S, T** — 5 floors. Floor 1 = ground, Floor 5 = top.

1. R lives on floor 2.
2. Q lives immediately above R.
3. T lives on the top floor.
4. P lives below S.
5. S does not live on floor 4.

**Questions:**
- (a) Who lives on floor 1?
- (b) Floor of P?
- (c) Who lives immediately below T?

---

## 4. ✍️ Quick Fire (5 min)

6. Step I rule: smallest number left. Input: 54 12 89 23 67. Step I = ?
7. Rank 15th from top, 20th from bottom. Total students?
8. A @ B means A is mother of B. A @ B # C means B is father of C. A @ B # C — A is ___ of C.
   - a) Mother  b) Grandmother  c) Sister  d) Aunt

---

## ✅ Answers

**Puzzle 1 — Circular (clockwise):** Q(5) — P(1) — R(2) — S(3) — T(4)

- Q immediate left of P → Q at 5, P at 1.
- R opposite Q → R at 2.
- S between R and T clockwise → S(3), T(4).
- T not neighbour of P ✓

- (a) Opposite P = **T**
- (b) Immediate right of Q = **P**
- (c) Neighbours of S = **R and T**

**Puzzle 2:**
- R = 2, Q = 3, T = 5. S not on 4 → S = 1. P below S → P = 4.

- (a) Floor 1 = **S**
- (b) P = **floor 4**
- (c) Below T = **P**

**Quick Fire:**
6. **12** 54 89 23 67
7. 15 + 20 − 1 = **34**
8. A is mother of B, B is father of C → A is **grandmother** → **b**
