## ✍️ Practice (10 Q)

1. A is 8 years older than B. Sum of ages = 48. Age of A?
2. Present ratio A:B = 3:4. Sum = 49. Age of B?
3. Father is 4 times son. Sum = 50. Son's age?
4. 5 years ago, A was 3 times B. A present = 23. B present?
5. After 6 years, A will be 2 times B. A present = 20. B present?
6. A:B = 2:5. B is 15. A = ?
7. Father 36, son 6. After how many years father = 3 × son?
8. 3 years ago ratio A:B = 4:5. A present = 19. B present?
9. Average age of 3 friends = 20. Two are 18 and 22. Third age?
10. Ram is 5 years older than Shyam. 3 years ago Ram was 2× Shyam. Ram present?

---

## ✅ Answers

1. B=x, A=x+8. 2x+8=48 → x=20. A=**28**
2. B = 4/7 × 49 = **28**
3. 4x+x=50 → x=**10** (son)
4. 5 ago A=18. 18=3B ago → B ago=6. Present B=**11**
5. 20+6=26=2(B+6) → B+6=13 → B=**7**
6. A = 2/5 × 15 = **6**
7. (36+t) = 3(6+t) → 36+t=18+3t → 2t=18 → t=**9 years**
8. A ago=16. Ratio 4:5 → B ago=20. Present B=**23**
9. Third = 60 − 40 = **20**
10. R=S+5. 3 ago: (S+2)=2(S−3) → S+2=2S−6 → S=8. Ram=**13**
