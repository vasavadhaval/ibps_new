## SECTION A — Mini RC (Q1-5)

**Passage:** Cybersecurity teams in banks use firewalls and encryption to protect customer data. Phishing emails trick users into sharing OTP or passwords. Banks advise customers never to click suspicious links. Regular software updates close known security gaps. Awareness training for staff is as important as new technology.

1. Main idea?
   - a) Banks should stop online banking  b) Security needs tech + awareness  c) OTP is always safe  d) Firewalls are useless
2. Phishing mainly tricks users to:
   - a) Save money  b) Share OTP/passwords  c) Update software  d) Visit branch
3. What closes security gaps?
   - a) Ignoring updates  b) Software updates  c) Sharing PIN  d) Closing accounts
4. Synonym of **suspicious** (here): a) safe b) doubtful/shady c) happy d) official
5. Inference: Staff training is important because:
   - a) Tech alone is not enough  b) Banks should close  c) OTP is free  d) Firewalls fail always
