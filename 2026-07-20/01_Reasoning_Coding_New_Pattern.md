## ‚úÖ Answers

9. ACE=1+3+5=**9** ‚Üí **a) ACE** (BED=11, DOG=26)

**Set D:**
- red is blue = ka la pa  
- blue and green = pa sa ta ‚Üí **blue = pa** (common)
- green is yellow = ta ka na ‚Üí **is = ka** (common with first), **green = ta**, **yellow = na**, red=la, and=sa

10. **pa**
