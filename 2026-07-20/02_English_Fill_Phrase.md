## A. Fill in the Blanks (Q1-6)

1. He is senior ___ me. (than / to / from)
2. Success depends ___ hard work. (on / at / for)
3. She has been working ___ Monday. (since / for / from)
4. I prefer tea ___ coffee. (than / to / over)
5. He congratulated me ___ my success. (for / on / at)
6. Neither of the boys ___ present. (is / are / were)

## B. Phrase Replacement (Q7-12)

7. He **don't like** tea.  
   a) doesn't like  b) didn't liked  c) not like  d) no change

8. Please **return back** the book.  
   a) return  b) give back again  c) returned back  d) no change

9. She is **more taller** than her sister.  
   a) taller  b) most taller  c) more tall  d) no change

10. He is good **in** English.  
    a) at  b) on  c) with  d) no change

11. The news **are** shocking.  
    a) is  b) were  c) have  d) no change

12. One of my friends **live** in Pune.  
    a) lives  b) living  c) have lived  d) no change

---

## ✅ Answers

1. **to**  2. **on**  3. **since**  4. **to**  5. **on**  6. **is**
7. **a**  8. **a**  9. **a**  10. **a**  11. **a**  12. **a**

---

## 🆕 8 Phrases (yaad karo)

| Wrong | Right |
|---|---|
| senior than | senior **to** |
| prefer than | prefer **to** |
| congratulate for | congratulate **on** |
| return back | **return** |
| more taller | **taller** |
| good in English | good **at** English |
| news are | news **is** |
| neither … are | neither … **is** |

---

> **Aaj ka takeaway:** Fixed prepositions exam me bar-bar aate hain. Phrase replacement = redundant word hatao (back, more).
