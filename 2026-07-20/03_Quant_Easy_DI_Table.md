## Table — Students in 4 Classes

| Class | Boys | Girls |
|---|---|---|
| A | 40 | 30 |
| B | 35 | 45 |
| C | 50 | 20 |
| D | 25 | 35 |

---

## ✍️ Questions

1. Total students in Class A?
2. Total boys in all classes?
3. Total girls in all classes?
4. Which class has maximum students?
5. Boys : Girls in Class B?
6. Difference between boys in C and girls in C?
7. Average boys per class?
8. Girls in A and D together?

---

## ✅ Answers

1. 40+30 = **70**
2. 40+35+50+25 = **150**
3. 30+45+20+35 = **130**
4. A=70, B=80, C=70, D=60 → **B (80)**
5. 35:45 = **7:9**
6. 50−20 = **30**
7. 150/4 = **37.5**
8. 30+35 = **65**

---

## Speed Tips
1. Pehle total column mentally note karo.
2. Ratio simplify karo (÷5).
3. Average = sum ÷ count.
4. Calculator mat socho — rough paper pe clean steps.
