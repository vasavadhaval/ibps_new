## A. Phrase Replacement (Q1-6)

1. He is **more smarter** than his brother.  
   a) smarter  b) most smarter  c) more smart  d) no change

2. Please **discuss about** the plan.  
   a) discuss  b) discuss on  c) discussing about  d) no change

3. She **hardly never** comes late.  
   a) hardly ever  b) hard never  c) never hardly  d) no change

4. He is **capable to** do this work.  
   a) capable of doing  b) capable for  c) capable in  d) no change

5. The train **arrived at** the station on time.  
   a) no change  b) arrived to  c) arrived in  d) arrived for

6. I look forward **to meet** you.  
   a) to meeting  b) to meet with  c) for meeting  d) no change

## B. Error Detection (Q7-12)

7. (A) Each of the students / (B) have / (C) a book. / (D) No error
8. (A) He is junior / (B) than / (C) me. / (D) No error
9. (A) She insisted / (B) to go / (C) alone. / (D) No error
10. (A) The furniture / (B) are / (C) costly. / (D) No error
11. (A) He made / (B) a blunder mistake / (C) yesterday. / (D) No error
12. (A) Scarcely had he left / (B) when / (C) the phone rang. / (D) No error

---

## ✅ Answers

1. **a** (smarter — no “more”)
2. **a** (discuss — no “about”)
3. **a** (hardly ever)
4. **a** (capable of doing)
5. **a** (arrived at — correct)
6. **a** (look forward to + -ing)
7. (B) have→**has**
8. (B) than→**to**
9. (B) to go→**on going**
10. (B) are→**is**
11. (B) blunder mistake→**blunder** (or “a mistake”)
12. **(D) No error**

---

## 🆕 Fix List

| Wrong | Right |
|---|---|
| more smarter | smarter |
| discuss about | discuss |
| capable to | capable **of** |
| look forward to meet | look forward to **meeting** |
| junior than | junior **to** |
| furniture are | furniture **is** |
| blunder mistake | **blunder** / mistake |

---

> **Aaj ka takeaway:** Redundant words hatao (more, about, mistake with blunder). Preposition + gerund patterns yaad rakho.
