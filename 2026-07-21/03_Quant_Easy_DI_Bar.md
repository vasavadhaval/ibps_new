# 🟡 Quant — Easy DI (Bar Chart) | Day 28

> Bar DI = values compare + %. Target: **6+ / 8** in 20 min.

---

## Bar Data — Monthly Sales (units)

| Month | Sales |
|---|---|
| Jan | 40 |
| Feb | 60 |
| Mar | 50 |
| Apr | 80 |
| May | 70 |

*(Exam me bar graph hoga — yahan table form me same data.)*

---

## ✍️ Questions

1. Total sales (Jan–May)?
2. Highest sales month?
3. Difference: Apr − Feb?
4. Average monthly sales?
5. Feb sales as % of Apr? (approx / exact)
6. Which two months total = 110?
7. Sales in May more than Jan by how much % of Jan?
8. Ratio Jan : Mar?

---

## ✅ Answers

1. 40+60+50+80+70 = **300**
2. **April (80)**
3. 80−60 = **20**
4. 300/5 = **60**
5. (60/80)×100 = **75%**
6. **Jan+May = 110** (40+70) — also Feb+Mar=110 (60+50)
7. Increase = 30. (30/40)×100 = **75%**
8. 40:50 = **4:5**

---

## Tips
1. Pehle totals / max-min mark karo.
2. % = (part/whole)×100.
3. Ratio simplify (÷10).
4. Do options same total ho sakte hain — dono check.
