## A. Cloze (Q1-6)

Banks are ___ (1) digital services so that customers can transfer money ___ (2) visiting a branch. However, cyber fraud has also ___ (3). Customers must never share OTP ___ (4) anyone. RBI regulates payment systems to ensure ___ (5). Financial literacy is as important as ___ (6) upgrades.

4. a) to  b) with  c) for  d) by
5. a) danger  b) safety  c) delay  d) loss

## B. Vocab / Phrase (Q7-12)
