## 1. ✍️ Puzzle 1 — Linear (8 min)

**A, B, C, D, E, F** — 6 persons in a row facing North (left → right).

1. B sits 2nd from the left end.
2. F sits at the right end.
3. C sits immediate right of B.
4. A sits 2nd to the left of F.
5. D sits at the left end.

**Questions:**
- (a) Who sits between C and A?
- (b) Position of E (from left)?
- (c) Who sits immediate left of F?

---

## 2. ✍️ Puzzle 2 — Floor (8 min)

**P, Q, R, S, T** — 5 floors (Floor 1 = ground, Floor 5 = top).

1. Q lives on floor 3.
2. P lives immediately below Q.
3. T lives on the top floor.
4. R does not live on floor 1.
5. S lives below R.

**Questions:**
- (a) Who on floor 1?
- (b) Who on floor 4?
- (c) Floor of S?

---
