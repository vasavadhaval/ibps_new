## A. Error Detection (Q1-6)

1. (A) Neither of the answers / (B) are / (C) correct. / (D) No error
2. (A) He is senior / (B) than / (C) his boss. / (D) No error
3. (A) She congratulated me / (B) for / (C) my result. / (D) No error
4. (A) The furniture / (B) in this room / (C) are new. / (D) No error
5. (A) One of my friends / (B) have / (C) a car. / (D) No error
6. (A) I look forward / (B) to meet / (C) you soon. / (D) No error

## B. Fill in the Blanks (Q7-12)

7. Success depends ___ hard work. (on / at / for)
8. He has been here ___ Monday. (since / for / from)
9. I prefer coffee ___ tea. (than / to / from)
10. She is good ___ English. (in / at / on)
11. He was ___ tired that he slept. (so / such / very)
12. ___ honest man is trusted. (A / An / The)

---

## ✅ Answers

1. (B) are→**is**
2. (B) than→**to**
3. (B) for→**on**
4. (C) are→**is**
5. (B) have→**has**
6. (B) to meet→**to meeting**
7. **on**  8. **since**  9. **to**  10. **at**  11. **so**  12. **An**

---

## Quick Fix Sheet

| Wrong | Right |
|---|---|
| neither … are | neither … **is** |
| senior than | senior **to** |
| congratulate for | congratulate **on** |
| furniture are | furniture **is** |
| look forward to meet | look forward to **meeting** |
| good in English | good **at** English |
