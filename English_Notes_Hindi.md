## Part A — Zaroori Grammar Rules

### 1. Subject-Verb Agreement
- Singular subject → singular verb (He **goes**), Plural subject → plural verb (They **go**).
- "Each, Every, Everyone, Nobody" → hamesha **singular verb**.
- "Either...or / Neither...nor" → verb **paas wale** subject se match karta hai.
- Collective nouns (team, family) → usually singular verb.

### 2. Tenses (kaal)
| Tense | Structure | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Present Simple | V1 / V5(+s) | He plays |
| Present Continuous | is/am/are + V-ing | He is playing |
| Present Perfect | has/have + V3 | He has played |
| Past Simple | V2 | He played |
| Past Perfect | had + V3 | He had played |
| Future | will + V1 | He will play |

### 3. Articles (a / an / the)
- **a** → consonant sound se pehle (a book, a university [yu-sound]).
- **an** → vowel sound se pehle (an apple, an hour [silent h]).
- **the** → specific/unique cheez (the sun, the Ganga).

### 4. Prepositions (common)
- **in** → mahina/saal/badi jagah (in 2026, in India)
- **on** → din/date/surface (on Monday, on the table)
- **at** → time/chhoti jagah (at 5 PM, at the door)

### 5. Common Error Areas (exam me aate hain)
- "One of the + **plural noun** + **singular verb**" → One of the boys **is** here.
- "Each of / Either of / Neither of + plural noun + singular verb".
- Uncountable nouns (information, advice, furniture, news) → **singular**, no plural 's'.
- "Since" → point of time ke saath; "For" → duration ke saath.

## Part B — Reading Comprehension (RC) — सबसे ज़रूरी 🔥

**Strategy:**
1. Pehle **questions** par nazar daalo, fir passage padho (kya dhoondhna hai pata ho).
2. Tone/main idea wale question last me karo.
3. **Vocabulary (synonym/antonym)** wale questions sabse easy — pehle karo.
4. Apni opinion mat lagao — **sirf passage me jo likha hai** uske aadhaar par answer.

**Tip:** Easy/factual passage pehle, difficult/economy-based passage chhod do agar time kam ho.

---

## Part C — Vocabulary (roz 10 words)

- Roz **10 naye words** + unke synonym/antonym yaad karo.
- Newspaper (The Hindu / Indian Express editorial) ya app se padho.
- Word ko **sentence me** use karo, tabhi yaad rahega.
- Common confusing pairs: affect/effect, accept/except, advice/advise, principal/principle.

---

## Part D — Para Jumbles

- **Pehla vakya** dhoondho: jo independent ho, kisi cheez ko introduce kare.
- "It, This, They, He, These" se shuru hone wale vakya **pehle nahi** aate (ye kisi cheez ko refer karte hain).
- Time/sequence words (then, after, finally) se order pakdo.

---

## Part E — Cloze Test & Fill in Blanks

- Pehle **poora passage** ek baar padho (context samajho).
- Grammar (article, preposition, tense) + meaning dono dekho.
- Blank ke aas-paas ke words se clue lo.

## General English Tips
- Roz **1 RC + 1 grammar topic** practice.
- Editorial padhne se RC, vocab, grammar — teeno improve hote hain.
- Negative marking hai — guess mat karo, sirf confident answer do.
