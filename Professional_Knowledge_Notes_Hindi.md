## 1. Kyun Zaroori Hai?

IT Officer Prelims me **4 sections** hain — teeno aptitude ke saath **Professional Knowledge** bhi mandatory hai.
- Har section ka **alag sectional cut-off** hota hai.
- PK me cut-off aksar **zyada** hota hai (marks me 20-28 range me, exam year par depend).
- 20 minute me 25 questions = **~48 sec per question** — theory + MCQ practice dono chahiye.

## 5. PK Exam Strategy (20 min)

1. Pehle **easy/definite** questions karo (full forms, basic definitions).
2. SQL output / logic questions — confident ho tabhi.
3. Guess kam — negative marking **0.25** hai.
4. Target: **15+ sahi** (30+ marks) safe zone; **18+ sahi** (36+ marks) strong.
