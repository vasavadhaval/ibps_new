# 8-Week Study Plan (2 Mahine) — IBPS IT Officer Prelims

> **Goal:** 55+ marks | **Focus:** Reasoning (high) → English (medium) → Maths (sirf cut-off)
> Roz ~2.5–3 ghante. Har hafte 6 din padhai, 1 din revision + mock.

---

## Daily Time Split (suggestion)

| Block | Time | Kaam |
|---|---|---|
| Block 1 | 60 min | Reasoning (naya topic + practice) |
| Block 2 | 45 min | English (grammar/vocab/reading) |
| Block 3 | 30 min | Maths (sirf easy topic) |
| Block 4 | 20 min | Error notebook revision |

---

## WEEK 1 — Foundation (Reasoning + English start)
- **Reasoning:** Inequality (<, >, =), Coding-Decoding, Alphanumeric Series
- **English:** Parts of Speech, Tenses (basic), Daily 10 new words
- **Maths:** Simplification (BODMAS), Approximation
- **Weekend:** Topic-wise tests; ek mini-mock.

## WEEK 2 — Reasoning Puzzles start
- **Reasoning:** Syllogism, Direction & Distance, Blood Relation
- **English:** Subject-Verb Agreement, Articles, Error Detection basics
- **Maths:** Number Series, Quadratic Equations
- **Weekend:** Reasoning sectional test + analysis.

## WEEK 3 — Puzzle mastery (most important)
- **Reasoning:** Linear Seating Arrangement, Box/Floor Puzzles
- **English:** Sentence Improvement, Preposition rules
- **Maths:** Simple Interest, Percentage (basic)
- **Weekend:** 1 full mock test. Galtiyan note karo.

## WEEK 4 — Puzzle + Order/Ranking
- **Reasoning:** Circular Seating, Order & Ranking, Data Sufficiency
- **English:** Cloze Test, Para Jumbles
- **Maths:** Profit & Loss (basic), Average
- **Weekend:** Sectional test (Reasoning + Maths).

---
### 🔁 Halfway Check (End of Week 4)
Ab tak Reasoning ke major topics ho jaane chahiye. Mock me Reasoning me **25+ marks** aane lagne chahiye. Agar nahi, toh Week 5 me Reasoning revision zyada.

---

## WEEK 5 — Speed building
- **Reasoning:** Mixed puzzles (timed), Input-Output (agar time ho)
- **English:** Reading Comprehension (RC) practice
- **Maths:** Ratio & Proportion, Age problems (basic)
- **Weekend:** 2 mock tests.

## WEEK 6 — Accuracy + DI (easy)
- **Reasoning:** Coding-Decoding (new pattern), Inequality (coded)
- **English:** Fill in the Blanks, Phrase Replacement
- **Maths:** Data Interpretation (sirf Table & Bar Graph — easy DI)
- **Weekend:** Full mock + deep analysis.

## WEEK 7 — Revision + Test series
- **Reasoning:** Sabhi topics ka revision + weak areas
- **English:** Vocab + Grammar revision
- **Maths:** Easy topics revision (Simplification, Series, SI/Percentage)
- **Weekend:** 3 mock tests is hafte.

## WEEK 8 — Final Polish
- Roz **1 full mock** + analysis (4-5 mocks total).
- Sirf **Error Notebook** revise karo, naya topic mat lo.
- Exam strategy fix karo: pehle Reasoning → English → Maths.
- Achhi neend, calm mind. ✅

---

## Exam Day Order (yaad rakho)
1. **English (15-18 min)** — fresh dimaag, easy marks
2. **Reasoning (45 min)** — yahan time do, max marks
3. **Maths (35-40 min)** — sirf easy/known questions karo, cut-off clear karo, baaki chhod do

> **Golden Rule:** Negative marking hai (-0.25). Sirf wahi question karo jisme **confident** ho. Guess mat karo.
